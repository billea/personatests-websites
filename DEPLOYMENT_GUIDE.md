## 🎯 Method 1: Netlify Drag & Drop (FASTEST - 5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Deployment Folder
1. **Create a new folder** on your desktop called `vibecheck-deploy`
2. **Copy these files** from `C:\Users\durha\Project\Testing website\` to the new folder:
   - `index.html`
   - `script.js`
   - `styles.css`
   - `privacy.html`
   - `terms.html`
   - `_redirects`

### Step 2: Deploy to Netlify
1. **Go to**: https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. **Drag the entire `vibecheck-deploy` folder** onto the Netlify drop zone
3. **Wait 30-60 seconds** for upload and deployment
4. **Get your live URL**: Something like `https://amazing-cupcake-123456.netlify.app`

### Step 3: Customize Your URL (Optional)
1. **Click "Site Settings"** in your Netlify dashboard
2. **Go to "Site Information"**
3. **Click "Change site name"**
4. **Enter**: `vibecheck-personality` or similar
5. **Your new URL**: `https://vibecheck-personality.netlify.app`

## 🎯 Method 2: Vercel (Alternative)

### Step 1: Prepare Files
Use the same `vibecheck-deploy` folder from Method 1

### Step 2: Deploy to Vercel
1. **Go to**: https://vercel.com
2. **Sign up/Login** (free account)
3. **Click "New Project"**
4. **Select "Browse All Templates"** → **"Browse Files"**
5. **Upload your folder**
6. **Click "Deploy"**
7. **Get your live URL**: Something like `https://vibecheck-abc123.vercel.app`
